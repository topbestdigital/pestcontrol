About

Welcome to BABA pest control India Pvt. Ltd.

        Baba Pest Control is an ISO 9001: 2015 certified, leading fumigation agency registered with “Plant Protection Quarantine & Storage” Ministry of agriculture & cooperation, Govt. of India Faridabad with our reorganization to all important ports such as Australia, Vietnam, Russia, and any other ports globally. We are authorized to undertake fumigation with Aluminium Phosphide and Methyl Bromide. We are having our presence at all measure ports in India such as Kandla, Mundra, Pipavav, Mumbai and south Indian ports Chennai, Kochi, Vaizak, Krishnapatnam etc. We are authorized fumigation agency and are engaged in providing quality services to our customers.

        BABA Pest Control, an ISO 9001-2015, was established in 1997 with its initial working in the filed of Domestic pest control and other disinfestations services. Latter in the year 2007 it stood in the field with its reorganization by the Director of plant protection Quarantine and storage and internationally identity with the authorization from “Australian Quarantine & Inspection services Australia.”
Baba Pest Control (BPC) is having its branch at Mumbai, Maharashtra, Gandhidham, Mundra, Vapi, Hazira and Pipavav, Gujrat. BPC is having its presence at Chennai, Vizag, Krishnapatnam, Tuticorin etc also.

MEMBER

        Baba Pest Control is a member of IPSA (Integrated Pest Solution Association) and Pest Control Association of India & abide by the standard set by the association to maintain the Quality of Work.

AIM

         To provide Quality Services with safety to our clients at international standard to meet the NSPM-12, ISPM-15 Standard to stood firm in international market of fumigation services.
